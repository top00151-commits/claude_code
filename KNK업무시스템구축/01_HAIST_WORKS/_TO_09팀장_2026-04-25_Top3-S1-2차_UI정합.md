## 4. INSERT/UPDATE 핸들러 5건 (main.py L4727~4995)

| 라우트 | 트랜잭션 | history |
|---|---|---|
| POST /sales/quotations | quotations INSERT (status=DRAFT, quote_no QT-YYYYMM-#### 자동) | (견적 — 미해당) |
| POST /sales/orders | quotations.status=CONFIRMED **+** orders INSERT (SO-YYYYMM-####, status=CONFIRMED) **+** history(DRAFT→CONFIRMED) | 1건 |
