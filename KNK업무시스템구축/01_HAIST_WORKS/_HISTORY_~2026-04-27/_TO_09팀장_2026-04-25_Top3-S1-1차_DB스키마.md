## 2. 8 enum 정의 위치 (`orders.status` CHECK constraint)

```sql
status TEXT DEFAULT 'CONFIRMED'
       CHECK(status IN ('DRAFT','QUOTED','CONFIRMED','IN_PRODUCTION',
                        'SHIPPED','PARTIAL_RECEIPT','PAID','CLOSED','CANCELLED'))
```
- 발주서 §S1 8 enum 8/8 + CANCELLED 1종 추가 = 9개. MVP는 4단계만 활성, 나머지는 enum만 정의(시안 §3).
- `quotations.status` (DRAFT/QUOTED/CONFIRMED/CANCELLED), `production_orders.status` (PLANNED/IN_PRODUCTION/READY_TO_SHIP/DONE/CANCELLED) 도 CHECK constraint.
